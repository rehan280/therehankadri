### What This Tool Does

This YouTube timestamp generator creates timestamp links and chapter text from any YouTube video URL.

Enter a video URL and a time (in seconds or MM:SS). Get a direct link that starts playback at that exact moment — plus formatted timestamp text you can paste into your description.

---

### Why YouTube Timestamps Are One of the Highest-ROI Things You Can Add to a Video

YouTube chapters — built from timestamps in your description — show up as orange markers on the video progress bar.

Here's why that matters: when your video shows chapters in Google search results, it gets a rich result with jump links. Viewers can click directly to the chapter that answers their question.

More importantly: YouTube shows chapter timestamps as individual results in search. A single video can occupy multiple spots on a Google search page if its chapters match different search queries.

This means a 20-minute video with 6 well-named chapters can effectively rank for 6 different searches — not just one.

Adding timestamps is one of the highest-leverage things you can do for an existing video's search visibility.

**Step 3: Get your timestamp link and text**

**Step 4: Add chapters to your description**

Paste the timestamp text into your YouTube description starting at 00:00. YouTube automatically converts them into chapter markers if formatted correctly.

### Timestamp Formatting Rules for YouTube Chapters

YouTube is strict about how timestamps need to be formatted to activate chapters:

- The first timestamp must be 0:00
- You need at least 3 timestamps total
- Each timestamp must have a label after it
- Timestamps must be in chronological order

Example:
```
0:00 Introduction
1:45 Step 1 — Setting Up
4:30 Step 2 — The Main Process
9:15 Common Mistakes to Avoid
13:00 Final Results
```

This tool generates timestamps in the correct format so you don't have to remember the rules.

### Frequently Asked Questions

**Do YouTube timestamps help with SEO?**
Yes. Chapter timestamps create rich results in Google search and can get your video multiple placements in search results for different queries.

**How many timestamps do I need for YouTube chapters?**
Minimum 3. YouTube recommends more for longer videos. A good rule is one chapter per 2 to 3 minutes of content.

**Can I add timestamps to old videos?**
Yes. Edit the description of any existing video and add timestamps. YouTube will activate chapters within a few hours.

**Do timestamps affect YouTube watch time?**
Timestamps let viewers skip to the sections they care about, which can reduce watch time per session. But they increase overall video utility and return visits — worth the tradeoff.
