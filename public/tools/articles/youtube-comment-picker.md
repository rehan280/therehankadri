## Why you need a proper random picker for YouTube giveaways

Here's the problem with manual comment picking: it's impossible to do fairly.

If your video has 500 comments and you scroll through picking "randomly," you're actually biased toward comments you saw longer, comments near the top, or comments from names you recognized. Your audience knows this.

A fair giveaway builds trust. A random picker that fetches ALL comments (not just the ones visible on screen) and shuffles them with an actual algorithm is the only genuinely fair method.

That's what this tool does.

## How to run a fair YouTube giveaway with this tool

**Step 1:** Post your giveaway video or choose an existing video for the giveaway. Pin a comment explaining the rules so viewers know how to enter (like, comment, or specific phrase required).

**Step 2:** Wait for entries to come in. Give your giveaway at least 24-48 hours to collect comments.

**Step 3:** Paste your video URL into the comment picker tool.

**Step 4:** The tool fetches the comments via YouTube's API. This captures comments from across the entire video, not just the top-loaded ones.

**Step 5:** Hit "Pick Winner" and the tool randomly selects from the pool.

**Step 6:** Screenshot or record the result and share it with your audience. Showing the process publicly makes the giveaway more credible.

## Can you filter comments?

This depends on your giveaway rules. If you required commenters to use a specific word or phrase to enter, you'd want to filter for that.

Check the tool's current filtering options when you use it. Filtering by keyword ensures only valid entries are included in the draw.

## Things to watch out for with YouTube comment giveaways

Some viewers use bot accounts to spam comments and improve their chances. YouTube catches and removes many of these, but not all.

If you see suspiciously repetitive comments from accounts with no history, use your judgment on whether to include them.

A clean, public picking process protects you from accusations of favoritism regardless of who wins.

## Frequently asked questions

**Does the tool fetch all comments or just the first page?**
It fetches comments via the YouTube Data API, which allows access beyond what's visible on screen. Large comment sections may have limits based on API quotas.

**Can I use this for Instagram or TikTok comments?**
This specific tool is for YouTube comments only. It uses YouTube's API.

**What if my video has hundreds of thousands of comments?**
Very large comment sections may be limited by API response limits. For giveaways, consider filtering by a specific entry phrase to reduce the pool to genuine participants.

**Is the random selection actually random?**
Yes. The tool uses algorithmic shuffling, not manual selection.

**Can I run the picker multiple times to select multiple winners?**
Yes. Run it again for each additional winner you want to pick.
